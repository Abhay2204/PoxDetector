🔍 How This Program Works

📸

1. Upload an Image

Users can upload a clear image of the affected skin area. The image should be in JPG, PNG, or JPEG format.

🧠

2. AI Model Analyzes

Our TensorFlow Lite model processes the image, resizing it to 224x224 pixels and extracting key features for classification.

🔬

3. Disease Detection

The model compares patterns with a medical dataset to determine whether the skin condition is Chickenpox, Measles, Monkeypox, or Normal.

📊

4. Result & Treatment

It displays the disease name, symptoms, and possible treatments, providing valuable guidance for further medical consultation.
